About Me...

Hello! I'm Jihee, a senior double majoring in Statistics and Data Science at the University of California, Berkeley.

From working with Facebook's Financial Business Analytics team this summer, building statistical models with UC faculty using SARIMA models, and conducting strategy consulting for Google with Berkeley ABA, I hold a demonstrated passion for data applications in various fields.

This semester, as a Finalist in UC Berkeley's California Actuarial League's 13th Annual Case Competition in both Retirement and Health & Benefits tracks and a data science consultant at UC Berkeley's Data Science Society, my comprehension and joy of computing has deepened.

I believe in using data science, statistics, and computer science science to emerge new discoveries that spark value in our world, and am looking to practice applying my knowledge in the actuarial and data science fields. Here you will find projects and experiences I have explored at Cal. Enjoy!
